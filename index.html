About this blog

Pricing Intelligence Blog covers pricing strategy, competitive intelligence, dynamic pricing, and market analysis for e-commerce and SaaS businesses.

Articles are published regularly and cover both tactical how-tos and big-picture strategic thinking around modern pricing.

This blog is part of an ongoing effort to make pricing intelligence accessible to growth-stage companies that can't yet afford enterprise tools.
